Goal: Design a single-page, highly interactive, and visually engaging homepage.

Responsiveness: Must be fully responsive and look amazing on both desktop and mobile devices.
2. Content & Section Breakdown:
Section Title
Content & Purpose
Visual Focus
Header/Hero

Bold, full-width background (e.g., a subtle dark parallax image of open books or celestial bodies).
Feature 1: AI Assistant "Novia"
Dedicated card/section introducing Novia, the AI. Capabilities: Book suggestions, answering reading queries, and functioning as an audio book narrator.

A dynamically styled grid or carousel showing 6-8 popular genres (e.g., Sci-Fi, Fantasy, Mystery, Romance). Each genre card must have an engaging icon or cover placeholder.

Explain that users can find the books online, read a free preview, or find an external link to purchase/read the full book.
